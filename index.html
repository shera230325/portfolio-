Research & AI Projects

Deep Learning-Based Soil Nutrient Profiling

Team Lead (5 members) · 2024 – 2025 · 1st Place, National Innovation & Technology Competition (600+ teams)

Built and trained a CNN from scratch on 5,000+ field photographs I personally collected and labelled — 91.4% classification accuracy on a held-out test set of 1,000+ images. Exported the model to run fully offline on a basic Android phone, built for villages with no internet where farmers pay ₹500 per lab test.

GitHub: soil-nutrient-cnn →

NLP for Indic Languages — Transformer Fine-Tuning

Independent Research · 2024 – Present

Fine-tuning Transformer models (HuggingFace) for Tamil and other Indic languages — 250M+ Tamil speakers currently have minimal access to functional NLP tools. Built a preprocessing pipeline that automated repetitive annotation, significantly cutting manual labelling time. Findings in preparation for submission to a student NLP workshop.

GitHub: Tamil-Indic-NLP →

Multi-Spectral Satellite Image Classification

Independent Project · 2023 – 2024

Built a K-Means pipeline classifying land use and vegetation cover from public multi-spectral satellite imagery across 12 zones — 87% average cluster coherence (silhouette scoring). Three zones showed measurable tree-cover loss, forming the basis of my ongoing environmental monitoring research.

GitHub: satellite-landcover-kmeans →

AI Business Intelligence Dashboard — Crop Price Forecasting (Tamil)

Independent Design Project · 2024 – 2025

Designed a dashboard pulling real-time climate and soil data to generate crop price forecasts for Tamil Nadu farmers and traders — output entirely in Tamil, as no district-level tool in the local language previously existed.

GitHub: crop-price-dashboard-tamil →

AI-Powered Earth Observation for Environmental Crisis Detection

Independent Research · 2026 – Present

Analysing public satellite datasets over coal mining regions (Jharkhand, Chhattisgarh) to detect underground coal fires and land subsidence using thermal anomaly and interferometric analysis — working toward an early-warning system before communities are displaced. Currently in dataset collection and exploratory analysis; initial findings expected by end of 2026.

GitHub: coal-subsidence-reduction →
